How to Get Dragon Goddess Fast

Follow these steps in order. Skipping any will waste your time — especially the Sea 2 unlock.

01

Hit Level 12,500 + Ascension 5

You cannot start the Sea 2 unlock without reaching Level 12,500 and Ascension Level 5. Use AoE fruits (Quake, Flame, Ice), follow quest NPC chains, and redeem 2x EXP boost codes.

Required Gate
02

Unlock Sea 2 via Ancient Fragments

Head to World Island in Sea 1 and speak to the Cartographer NPC. Collect 2 Ancient Fragments from 20 locations across 12 Sea 1 islands. Fragments spawn every 1m 30s and despawn after 5 min.

Pro Tip: Use Private Server
03

Max Out Your Stats Before Farming

Sea Beasts have ~1.25 trillion HP. Equip the best titles, races, and clan bonuses. Bless and Enchant everything. Keep your bounty high — higher bounty = faster Sea Beast spawns.

Critical for Speed
04

Sail to Open Sea & Hunt Sea Beasts

Sea Beasts don't spawn on land. Sail into the Open Sea of Sea 2. Spawns are global and random — join Discord alert channels so your squad gets notified the moment one appears.

Target: Kraken & Sea Serpent
05

Defeat & Loot — Repeat Until Drop

Each kill guarantees a Bloodline Stone + Clan Reroll. The Sea Serpent drops at 2.08% vs Kraken's 0.96%. The April 19 hotfix buffed the rate — now is the best time to farm.

Best Source: Sea Serpent

(Optional) Buy It Directly with Robux

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common Dragon Goddess questions.

Do Sea Beasts spawn on a timer? ▾
No. Spawns are completely random and global — no fixed timer. Join a Discord with spawn notifications and AFK sail in open sea while you wait.
Can I farm Dragon Goddess in Sea 1? ▾
No. The Kraken and Sea Serpent only appear in Sea 2. Complete the Ancient Fragment questline from the Cartographer NPC on World Island first (requires Lvl 12,500 + Ascension 5).
What does the Dragon Queen title do? ▾
It provides +97.5% damage — the highest damage title in the game. It drops from the same Sea Beasts, so you can farm both simultaneously for the ultimate endgame loadout.
Is buying Dragon Goddess with Robux worth it? ▾
At 1,499 Robux standalone it guarantees the sword instantly. The 4,299 Robux bundle adds The World, Cosmic Being, and Great Mage — better value if you want multiple Sea 2 items.
What else drops from Sea Beasts? ▾
Every kill guarantees a Bloodline Stone and Clan Reroll. Chance drops include the Blossom Outfit, Kraken Armor, and Dragon Goddess. The Dragon Queen title also drops here.
